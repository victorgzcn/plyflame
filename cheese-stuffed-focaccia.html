Cheese Stuffed Focaccia

A creamy mixed cheese filling adds a special touch to focaccia.

üìã Ingredients

Dough:
4 1/2 Cups All-purpose Flour
1 Tablespoon Instant Yeast
1 Teaspoon Salt
2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
1 1/2+ Cups Warm Water
Filling:
1 1/2 Cups Diced Gorgonzola Dolce Cheese
1 1/2 Cups Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
1 Cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
1/4 Cup Finely Chopped Fresh Basil
Topping:
1 Teaspoon Coarse Sea Salt
2 Teaspoons Fresh Chopped Rosemary
3 Tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil

üë©‚Äçüç≥ Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, add 1 1/2 cups warm water to the yeast and stir until dissolved.
  2. Add the salt, olive oil and flour and stir until mixed, adding a teaspoon or two of more water if needed to create a soft dough.
  3. Transfer dough to a floured surface and knead by hand until the dough is smooth, about 7 minutes.
  4. Divide the dough into two equal sized balls of dough and cover to rest 30 minutes.
  5. Line a large flat baking sheet with parchment paper.
  6. Roll one ball of dough into a circle about 14 to 15 inches wide and place on the prepared baking pan.
  7. In a bowl, mix together the three types of cheese and fresh basil.
  8. Top the shaped circle with the filling mixture, leaving a 1 inch border all around.
  9. Roll out the second ball of dough the same size and place over the filling.
  10. Press the edges of the two circles of dough together firmly to encase the filling.
  11. Cover the focaccia with kitchen towels and rest 45 minutes.
  12. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
  13. Use your finger tips to create dimples over the top on the dough, then drizzle the top with the oil, and sprinkle with the sea salt and rosemary.
  14. Bake focaccia around 30 minutes until golden brown.
  15. Allow the focaccia to cool 10 minutes, then slice into wedges or strips and serve warm.
